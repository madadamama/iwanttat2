An interview with

How did you become interested in tattooing?

i had always been interested visual art & tattoos growing up but never had plans to become a tattoo artist. however, in college i was doing a lot of dot work drawings i thought would be cool as handpoke tattoos and also started getting tattooed by a local artist who was self taught and saw that i could teach myself too. from there, i started tattooing myself & friends to learn and progressively got better.

What’s one thing you wish another tattoo artist told you before you started tattooing?

i wish another artist emphasized the importance of considering how a tattoo is going to age overtime and that the client has an informed understanding of that in relation to the tattoo they’re getting.

Do you have a dream tattoo, if so, what does it look like?

i don’t think i have a “dream” tattoo but i’m really excited to see how the composition of all my tattoos changes over time as i add to my collection.
